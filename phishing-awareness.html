Frequently Asked Questions

My bank sent an email asking me to verify my login details. Should I click the link?

No. Legitimate organizations will never ask for your password or full login details via email. If you are concerned, navigate to their official website by typing the address directly into your browser or use their official app.

What should I do if I accidentally clicked a phishing link?

Disconnect your device from the internet immediately to prevent further communication with the malicious server. Run a full antivirus scan. Change the password for the account that was targeted, as well as any other accounts that use the same password.
