Frequently Asked Questions

Should I hide my Wi-Fi network's name (SSID)?

While it may seem like a good idea, hiding your SSID offers very little real security. Attackers with basic tools can still easily find hidden networks. It's far more important to focus on strong encryption (WPA3/WPA2) and a strong password.

Is it safe to do online banking on airport or hotel Wi-Fi?

It is very risky without protection. You should only perform sensitive activities like banking on public Wi-Fi if you are using a reputable VPN. The VPN creates an encrypted tunnel for your data, protecting it from others on the network.
