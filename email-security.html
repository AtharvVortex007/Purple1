Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to click "unsubscribe" on a spam email?

It can be risky. Clicking "unsubscribe" on a legitimate marketing email is fine. But on a spam email, it just confirms to the spammer that your email address is active, which can lead to even more spam. It's safer to just mark the email as spam.

My friend sent me an attachment. Is it safe to open?

If you were expecting it, it's likely safe. If it's unexpected and has a generic message like "Check this out!", be cautious. Your friend's account could have been hacked. It's always best to confirm with them through a different method (like a text message) before opening it.
