Frequently Asked Questions

Is deleting a file and emptying the Recycle Bin enough to make it unrecoverable?

No. In most cases, this action only removes the pointer to the file, but the actual data remains on the hard drive until it is overwritten by new data. Forensic tools can often recover these "deleted" files.

What is the difference between digital forensics and cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is primarily about prevention—building walls and defenses to stop attacks. Digital forensics is primarily about reaction—investigating what happened *after* an incident has occurred to find evidence and determine the cause.
