Frequently Asked Questions

Should I clear my browser cookies regularly?

Yes, clearing cookies periodically is good practice. It can protect your privacy by removing tracking data and can also help resolve some website performance issues. You can configure your browser to clear them automatically on exit.

Is it safe to use public Wi-Fi at a cafe or airport?

Public Wi-Fi networks are generally not secure. Other users on the same network could potentially intercept your data. Avoid logging into sensitive accounts (like banking) and always use a VPN (Virtual Private Network) on public Wi-Fi for an encrypted, secure connection.
