Frequently Asked Questions

Can my smart speaker listen to all my conversations?

Smart speakers are designed to listen only for their "wake word" (e.g., "Hey Google"). However, they can activate accidentally and record snippets of conversation. You can review and delete these recordings in your device's app settings.

Are cheap, no-name smart devices from online marketplaces safe?

They can be very risky. These devices often have poor security, may not receive updates, and could even be sending your data to unknown servers. It's safer to stick with reputable, well-known brands.
