Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need antivirus software on my phone?

While phones have strong built-in security, reputable antivirus apps can add another layer of protection, particularly on Android. They can scan for malicious apps and block phishing sites.

Is it safe to use public USB charging stations?

Be cautious. This practice, known as "juice jacking," could potentially allow a compromised port to install malware or steal data from your device. It's safer to use your own power adapter and plug it into a standard electrical outlet.
