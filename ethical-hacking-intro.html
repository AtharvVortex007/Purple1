Frequently Asked Questions

Is ethical hacking legal?

Yes, it is legal as long as you have explicit, written permission from the owner of the system you are testing. Without permission, it is a crime.

Do I need to be a programmer to be an ethical hacker?

While not strictly required for beginner concepts, strong programming and scripting skills become increasingly important as you advance in the field. They allow you to understand code, find flaws, and automate tasks.
