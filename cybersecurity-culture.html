Frequently Asked Questions

How often should security awareness training happen?

Security awareness should be continuous, not a once-a-year event. Regular, bite-sized training, monthly newsletters, and periodic phishing simulations are far more effective at keeping security top-of-mind.

What is a "Security Champion"?

A Security Champion is a regular employee who is passionate about security and acts as a point of contact for their team. They help promote best practices and bridge the gap between the official security team and other departments.
