Frequently Asked Questions

Should I accept friend requests from people I don't know?

No. Accepting requests from strangers can expose your personal information to scammers and criminals. It's best to only connect with people you know and trust in the real world.

Are the links in direct messages (DMs) always safe?

No, you should be very cautious. Scammers often compromise accounts and then send malicious links to all of that person's friends. If a link looks suspicious, even from a friend, verify it with them through another method before clicking.
