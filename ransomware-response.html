Frequently Asked Questions

Does paying the ransom guarantee I'll get my files back?

No. There is no guarantee. The criminals may take your money and provide a faulty decryption key or no key at all. Paying also encourages them to continue attacking others.

Can ransomware infect cloud storage like Google Drive or Dropbox?

Yes. If your cloud storage is synced to an infected computer, the ransomware can encrypt the local files, and those encrypted versions can then be synced to the cloud, overwriting your clean copies.
